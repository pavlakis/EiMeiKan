Welcome - はじめまして

We practice the Japanese martial art of Aikido at the Faircharm Trading Estate in Leicester

This is a new club starting in June 7th 2013. Classes will be every Friday 7pm - 8.30pm

Our club is part of the United Kingdom Aikikai.

If you're interested in starting aikido or if you already train, you're welcome to come along and join us.

Beginners

If you are interested to try aikido, come along on one of our classes. The first class if FREE!

Wear loose clothing like a t-shirt and jogging bottoms.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is aikido?

    Aikido is a non-competitive Japanese martial art created in the early 1940's by Morihei Ueshiba. A good introductory text can be found on the aikido wikipedia page

  • What do I need to get started?

    Just come along to one of our classes. Wear loose clothing like a t-shirt and jogging bottoms. The first class is free.

    If you join the club it would be good to get a keikogi (also referred to as 'gi'). These are the same as judo or karate white uniforms. They cost around £20 and you can get them from any martial arts shop. We tend to use NineCircles.

  • How much does it cost?

    Classes cost £5 per lesson for adults and £3 for students and unwaged.

  • How can I join?

    If you do decide to join our club, you must join the UKA. Membership costs £32 per year (£25 for students/unwaged). Complete this membership form and give it to the instructor together with your payment. If you're paying by check, it will need to be payable to "Renshinkan Affiliations".

Instructors

Antonios Pavlakis 1st dan (shodan) has been studying aikido since 1997 and has a Sports England teaching qualification (CL1) through the British Aikido Board (BAB).

Antonis started Aikido at De Montfort University Aikido Club under the instruction of Dave Lenton sensei. In 1999, Dave Lenton sensei (4th dan) passed the club to Terry Mickowski sensei (4th dan) with whom he has been training ever since.

He often travels abroad to attend aikido classes and over the years has practiced with J. C. Aegerter shihan, N. Tamura shihan, C. Tissier shihan, Y. Kobayashi shihan, as well as the present Doshu.

He gets regular tuition from Philip Smith shihan (6th dan) from Ren Shin Kan dojo and other UKA shihan and shidoin.
